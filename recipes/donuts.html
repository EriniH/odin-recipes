Donuts

Description

A recipe for making classic donuts at home better than these you can by at stores. Fresh and fluffy donuts and doesn't absorb oil!

Aya Habib's Video

Ingredients (6 Donuts)

  • 270 g flour (2 1/4 cups)
  • 125 ml Milk (0.5 cup)
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tbsp acitive dry Yeast
  • 50 grams butter(1/4 cup)
  • 25g Sugar (2 tbsps)
  • Sprinkle of Salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla

Steps:

  1. Put the flour, the sugar, the yeast and the butter (at room temperature) into a big bowl.
  2. Add the vanilla and the egg
  3. Use a mixer to knead the dough at low speed to avoid flour ventilation
    4. You can also knead it manually. It is a sticky dough but don't try to add extra flour so that it stays fluffy.
  4. We add milk slowly while kneading
  5. Keep kneading for 10-15 minutes until the dough is soft
  6. Put the dough in a bowl with a wipe of oil and let it rest for 1-2 hours till the size doubles
  7. Roll the dough out to 1.5 cm thickness, cut into circles and cut into holes
  8. Let the donuts rest for other 30-45 minutes
  9. Prepare the oil. Using a heavy-bottomed pot heat oil to 160°C
  10. Fry the donuts for 2-3 minutes on each side then remove
