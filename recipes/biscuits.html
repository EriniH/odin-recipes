10 minute biscuits

Description

A recipe for making easy economical simple biscuits. The 10-minute biscuits recipe uses four ingredients that are in every house and is beginner friendly!

Aya Habib's Video

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 tsp Vanilla

Steps:

  1. add an egg, half cup of sugar and half cup of oil into a big bowl.
  2. Add a small tsp of vanilla and mix them all together till the sugar dissolves
  3. Add the flour and the baking powder and mix them together
  4. Shape the biscuits and add a coat of sugar to each of them
  5. Put in the oven at 180Â°C for 15-20 minutes
